Smite New God (Lancelot)

Aug 01st 2022 3 Min read

Lancelot, a Knight of the round table has joined Smite's roster alongside King Arthur.

Lancelot is an Assassin class perfect for the jungle role with his high mobility on horseback, he is also the first assassin to have a stance swap which has been requested by fans for some time.


Abilities:

  • ◉ Passive ability: Camelot's Quest

    • - Lancelot receives 1 stack for a god kill/assist and 1 stack for every 5 minion kills. (Max 50 stacks)

    - While Mounted, each stack increases movement speed.

    - While unmounted, each stack reduces basic attack damage from the front.


  • ◉ 1st Ability: Piercing Thrust

    • - Lancelot does a quick dash and thrust forwards with his lance. He deals damage to enemies in his path and those hit by the extending lance.


  • ◉ 2nd Ability: Skilled Strikes

    • - Lancelot slams his shield into ememies in a small area in front of him, dealing damage and knocking them back. He follows up this attack with a sweep of his lance dealing damage again and bonus damage to enemies that are further away.


  • ◉ 3rd Ability: Mount Up

    • - Lancelot mounts onto his trusty steed charging forwards with increased movement speed. While on his horse Lancelot gains a health shield that builds up over time.



    Mounted Abilites:


  • ◉1st Ability: Shining Gallop

    • - Lancelot sends off his horse to gallop ahead in a line as he dismounts. The horse fades away allowing it to pass through walls, dealing damage and slowing enemies in a larger area.


  • ◉2nd Ability: Crashing Dive

    • - Lancelot surges upwards off his horse, rooting all enemies nearby, before diving back down to deal damage to a large area.


  • ◉3rd Ability: Quick Turn

    • - Lancelot turns quickly while mounted to face the opposite direction.


  • ◉Ultimate Ability: The Grand Joust

    • - Lancelot rears up his horse as a large jousting area is created, crippling enemies in the vicinity. Lancelot charges ahead, dealing damage to all enemies hit and refreashing his mounted shield.
