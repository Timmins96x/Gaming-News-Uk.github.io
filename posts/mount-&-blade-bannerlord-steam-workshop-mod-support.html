Mount & Blade Bannerlord has released Steam Workshop Mod Support!

Oct 18th 2022 6 Min read


Mount & Blade Bannerlord released in early access in March 2022, The Developer's have none stop released updates for the game, but now it's almost time for the game to leave Early Access,(October 25th).


Taleworlds Entertainment (Bannerlords Developers,) deserve some credit for the dedication they have put into Mount & Blade, the game has changed alot since laungh, from bug fixes to refining the in-game systems such as, diplomacy, AI combat strategy, massive multiplayer Overhaul and last on the list is the ability for troops to hold banners.




Steam workshop mod support has been the most requested feature by fans for some time, it just makes life alot easier than using external Mods. Now with a click of the button they will automatically intergrate into your game.

Their is already some impressive mods on the workshop to download such as a map of Europe to conquer, Dismemberment, and even a Game Of Thrones mod, with plenty more to arrive im sure hopefully some will be imported from NexusMods, like the gunpowder mod and the roman mod.
