Stardew Valley Review (2022)

Sep 2nd 2022 4 Min read


Stardew Valley released in 2016 and was an overnight hit, astoundingly developed by a single person. On steam it is one of the highest reviewed games of all time, and at the time of writing is 10th on the top-sellers list, which is no easy feat for a 7 year old game.

This review is to show you what updates have been released, read on for more information.


Multiplayer:

Out of all the updates the most game changing is of course adding multiplayer, you can now play with friends even get married and have children, i don't think anyone expected such an update which made it all the better.

You can have a total of four players in one world, you all have your own houses to use, either build them or start a multiplayer server that gives you the houses free off the start of the game.

In multiplayer co-ordination is key, all players share the same purse. Going to bed and saving only works when all players go to bed at the same time. The multiplayer mode offers options to spread tasks as well, one person could farm, one could go mining and the other could fish etc.


New maps for farm:

In addition to the multiplayer update, there are now 6 new map options for your homestead, these not only look different but also bring in their own advantages and disadvantages.

  • Riverbed
  • Forest
  • Mountain Tops
  • Wilderness
  • Corners
  • Beach

New Quests and location:

Plenty of new quests have been added to the game since release. A main one is once you've collected all the items for the community centre, Willy the Fisherman invites you to his home. There you can repair his old boat, which unlocks a new area "Ginger Island".

At first it seems like a small island, but turns out to be a highly thought out area with its own farm with a bed and a crazy volcano.


Overall:

Stardew Valley, although it's been out for 7 years has not lost it's touch and still has thousands of players daily.

With all the new features it's understandable why this game hasn't died you can see the dedication from the solo developer, furthermore yes this game is still 100% worth playing in 2022, just check the reviews from other players on Steam...
