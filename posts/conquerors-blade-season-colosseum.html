Conqueror's Blade Season Colosseum

Sep 05th 2022 3 Min read

Conqueror's blade season Colosseum has released, bringing plenty of new content inspired by Ancient Rome, rise as a champion in the new colosseum mode a 6v6 pvp onslaught, fight through the ranks in a new seasonal campaign, New units inspired by Roman Gladiators, and special launch events.


Colosseum mode:


The main feature of the season is the Colosseum mode, inspired by the gladiator battles of Ancient Rome.

Fight in 6v6 battles armed with only two respawn's, one piece of artillery, and your own units. Lead the greatest gladiator's in the world to glory in this new arena and brave the trials and tribulations before a cheering crowd.


Gladiator Unit's:


Three new seasonal unit's inspired by Ancient Roman gladiator's are now available to unlock through the grindy seasonal challenges, each bringing unique tactics to the colosseum:

  • Dimachaeri- Available now, a staple of the colosseum's brutal battles, These gladiators are armed with two blades, which they defly use to defend and attack.

  • Myrmillones- Available now, trained from a young age, these fighters excel at combat and breaking down enemy defences with their sword and shield combo's.

  • Retiarii- Available October 3rd, the undisputed champions of the colosseum. They wield javelins and tridents, excellent mobility, and a secret curse.

Battle pass:


With every season comes a new battle pass filled with over 100 rewards based off Rome. Equip your units with the new Equites unit attire, and ride into battle with the new horse skin the "Triumphant", tier 100 offers the ultimate trophy the "Centurion attire".
