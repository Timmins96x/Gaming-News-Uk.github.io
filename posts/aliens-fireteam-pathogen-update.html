Aliens Fireteam Pathogen Update

Aug 25th 2022 4 Min read

Aliens fireteam is a co-op shooter released in 2021 by Cold Iron Studios , set in the Alien universe, unlike other Alien games fireteam took a full on action approach as opposed to the Alien survival horror games, the game reached top seller on Steam for the first week of its released.

Since the game released there have been a few updates but none really brought the player base back, it rapidly died due to the lack of content, the Pathogen update is the developers attempt to bring the game back to life.


The new update brings plenty of content:


  • 3 New single-player/co-op missions
  • 8 New weapons (Two for each class)
  • 21 New weapon Colours
  • 1 New perk for every class
  • 2 New outfits for each class
  • 6 New head accessories
  • 10 New decals
  • 10 New emotes
  • New weapon attachments
  • Pathogen Queen (New Boss)

The main part of the update being the extra story missions, with new areas to explore, the Pathogen enemy types and of course the Pathogen Queen.
