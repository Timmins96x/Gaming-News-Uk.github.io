The Day Before delayed until 2023

Aug 2nd 2022 6 Min read


The Day Before, the most wishlisted game on steam, and for good reason. Who Doesn't want to play a zombie apocalypse MMO?

The game has been delayed by nearly a year what was expected to release by June 21st, 2022 has been pushed back to March 1st, 2023 lots of disappointment by fans who are excited to play the game.

FUNTASTIC, the developers of the game have said the reason for the delay is due to upgrading the game to Unreal Engine 5.

It will be available on PC via Steam, Xbox Series X/S and PS5.

The Day Before, is a post apocalyptic massively-multiplayer online (MMO). It will be played in a third person perspective, mixing survival style games with combat, vehicles, and other intresting elements.

Many Gamers are calling it a mix of "The Division" & "The Last Of US".
