Rimworld Biotech Update

Oct 10th 2022 4 Min read

The third Rimworld expansion "Biotech", is only a few weeks away, releasing with Update 1.4. This is by far the biggest expansion so far and with a much fan-requested feature being added.


Starting Families:


It's finally happening, with Biotech expansion you can now conceive and bear children, though natural ways and artificial ways, meaning you cna create colonies to last generations.

With update 1.4 colonists can become pregnant, but you can also grow children in vats, and even partake in some Gene-modding to create colonists with superpowers or xenohumans, who have traits adapted specifically to alien enviroments.

The main responsibility of having children is to raise them well to get the best traits which will benefit the colony in the long run, you now have access to education to aid the children in becoming capable colonists.


New Mechanitor:


Update 1.4 also brings the "Mechanitor", a colonist who has a brain implant which allows them to command mechanoids telepathically. If you've played Rimworld before you know how difficult mechanoids can be to kill, now controlling them sounds like pure havoc...
