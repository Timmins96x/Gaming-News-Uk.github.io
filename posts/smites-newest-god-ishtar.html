Smite's New God (Ishtar)

Aug 23th 2022 3 Min read

Ishtar, the Goddess of fertility, love, sex and war has arrived to Smite expanding the already impressive roster.

Ishtar is a DPS Carry, an impressive one at that, her kit rewards close combat and aggression. She can gap close effectively and follow it up with light attacks and abilities that rewards her for being closer to the opposition than the traditional hunter boxing matches.


Unique Gameplay Features:


  • ◉ A basic attack stim with 3 options for different ways to buff herself.
  • ◉ 4 Different basic attacks within her kit.
  • ◉ A new type of targeting on a ranged attack
  • ◉ The ability to start an attack as Ishtar and move out from her, or choose to start at a range and have it move back towards her.
  • ◉ A short dash with a unique combo finish if it hits an enemy god.
  • ◉ An ultimate that sends out projectiles but then also returns them back to Ishtar regardless of location distance.

The time has come, the next Babalonian God is here and she wont be backing down from any fights. Players with the skill and courage to match Istvar's play style will certainly be rewarded!
