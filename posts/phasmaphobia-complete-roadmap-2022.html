Phasmaphobia complete roadmap (2022)

Aug 01th 2022 4 Min read

Phasmaphobia is a 4-player online co-op phychological horror, paranormal activity is on the rise, and its it's upto you and your team to gather evidence on what ghost it is.

Phasmophobia was released in 2020, it was the top seller on steam for over a month and is sitting nicely with over 300,000 positive reviews.


2022 Roadmap:

"Kinetic Games" the developer of Phasmaphobia are only a small team, but have actively been adding new content, here's everything expected to be added for the rest of 2022:


  • ◉ VR Rework
  • ◉ Unity Engine Update
  • ◉ New main menu board
  • ◉ Upgradable Equipment
  • ◉ New Equipment
  • ◉ New player models/animations
  • ◉ New levelling system
  • ◉ New Daily/weekly challenges
  • ◉ Player customisation

The developers also released this statement after posting the roadmap:

"Don't worry, other updates like Horror 2.0, new maps, map reworks and more are still planned and will be squeezed in or developed after these major updates."
