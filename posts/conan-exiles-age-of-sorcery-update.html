Conan Exiles Age Of Sorcery Update

Sep 01th 2022 6 Min read

Conan Exiles age of sorcery is the biggest update since the game released in 2017, bringing in a new sorcery class, Transmog system, many major revamp systems and even a battle pass.

Conan Exiles had one of the worst launches in history in 2017, constantly being kicked out of the server and far too many bugs. What made it so much worse was how long it took to fix the issues, but since then they have been hard at work and the game has noticably come a long way.


Age of sorcery as indicated by the title is the new sorcery mechanics, players can unlock sorcery powers by sacrificing their own bodies to permanant corruption effects as a trade off, which allows you to practice ritual magic, alchemy and spell casting, new workstations have also been added for you to build:

  • Thaumaturgy Bench
  • Chalk Circle
  • Sacrificial Stone
  • Shallow Grave
  • Transportory Stone

A complete revamp of perk and ability systems should make surviving Conan Exiles more interesting with more meaningful character progression choices. One of the new attributes is "authority" which increases the amount of Thralls you can command at once.

The building system has also been reworked and now centered around the construction hammer, which makes it so much easier on the eyes and to understand what to build.


Conan has joined the trend by adding a battle pass, quite unusual for a survival game. It consists of free rewards aswell the usual blocked rewards until you buy the premium version. The pass contains 60 levels in total, this season is all reflected on sorcery, you will receive challenges to advance levels.


The rewards are:

  • Armor Cosmetics
  • Weapon Cosmetics
  • Building Parts
  • Companions
  • Emotes
  • Decorations
  • War Paint
  • Mounts
  • Crom Coins (Premium Currency)
