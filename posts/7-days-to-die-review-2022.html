7 Days To Die Review (2022)

Aug 25th 2022 4 Min read

7 Days To Die is a zombie survival crafting game, it's been in alpha for almost 10 years surprisingly, although it's been out for a long time the game is still very much alive by the player base and the developers "Funpimps" releasing updates to this day.

Please note this review is based on the PC version of 7 Days To Die not the console version due to Funpimps having licensing issues on the console versions, which means they haven't been able to receive updates so i would stay clear.



The main purpose of 7 Days To Die is survive and gather resourses, i still play to this day with friends, O i highly recommend playing this with friends makes the game so much more fun. The first step is get yourself a weapon, find a home, build defences for the upcoming horde and later try to find/craft guns, ammo and even vehicles.



Since the Alpha release there have been multiple major updates containing a new Snow Biome, forging systems, new weapons, general graphical changes, smoother terrains and new zombie varients.



Overall:

7 Days To Die is 100% still worth playing especially with friends, a base building zombie apocalypse with survival aspects is a great combo, it has very positive reviews on steam and a large player base with no sign of stopping..

Pearce Timmins
