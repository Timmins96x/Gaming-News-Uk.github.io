Age of Empires IV Sea of Monsters Event

Aug 22th 2022 3 Min read

Age of Empires has been around since 1997, between then and now the games have become classics, still one of the best strategy games to date. With the release of Age of Empires IV in 2021 there have been no shortage of updates, with new features such as maps, cosmetics, balances and user friendly features.

Sea Of Monsters:

Season two brings the Sea of Monsters event, aswell as more set to be released before Season 3 arrives:


  • ◉ Age of Discovery Event (July 12th- July 26th)
  • ◉ By Stars And Sea Event (August 9th - August 30th)
  • ◉ Wonders of The Deep Event (September 1st - September 22nd)
  • ◉ Modern Monsters Event (October 4th - October 18th)

All will bring new challenges and cosmetics for you to unlock.


Season Two Update Notes:


  • ◉ Map preference system
  • ◉ Player colour picker
  • ◉ Remappable keys
  • ◉ Fully unlocked campaigns
