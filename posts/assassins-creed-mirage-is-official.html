Assassins Creed Mirage is official!

Sep 5th 2022 4 Min read

Ubisoft have finally announced that Assassins Creed Mirage is official, after all the countless fake leaks about Assassins Creed's setting we can all rest easy now.

Mirage is confirmed to release in 2023, although Ubisoft haven't given an exact date yet.

All release platforms will be PS5, PS4, Xbox Series x and Xbox One. It will also arrive on PC through the Ubisoft Store and the Epic Games Store.

Assassins Creed Mirage takes place in Baghdad, focusing on a younger Basim as the main character. It will be set in the year 861 The Golden Era, a few decades before Assassins Creed Valhalla.

For those of you who haven't played Valhalla without spoilers Basim was introduced as an important character of the story, highly recommend completing Valhalla before Mirage is released to get a good grasp on the story.

Ubisoft have said Mirage will go back to the roots and focus more on the stealth of being an Assassin, parkour is a big focusing point again on Mirage as well, seems like a similar approach to the fist iconic Assassins Creed games.

Overall i'm looking forward to what the game is trying to achieve, they have listened to the fans who have been with them since the first Assassins Creed and will be adding the classic stealth features the newer titles have been lacking.
