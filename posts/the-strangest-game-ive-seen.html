The Strangest game i've seen... (One Hour One Life)

Aug 22th 2022 3 Min read

One Hour One Life released in 2018, developed by a single person "Jason Rohrer", the game is classed as a survival massively-multiplayer online game, One Hour One Life was an incredibly popular game, the developer hasn't updated the game in a few years now but that hasn't stopped the fams from making their own modded versions thanks to Jason releasing the source code of the game.


What makes the game so unusual is the entire goal of it all, players have 60 minutes before they die of old age or quicker if you starve or are attacked by wildlife, even players can kill you. Your objective is to keep your family from dying out, you start as a new born baby born from another player who needs to look after you until your old enough to survive by yourself, which is unusual enough.

In your life span you ultimately want to build, farm and gather to make it easier and easier for the next generations to come, technically the game could go on for a very long time if a family line can last long enough to reach the modern age.
